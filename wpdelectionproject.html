The 2016 presidential candidates for the election are Donald Trump and Hillary Clinton. Hillary Clinton is representing the Democratic Party and Donald Trump is representing the Republican Party. Each are facing off in a race to the White House. The election will be held on November 8th.

General Facts About Trump

  1. He is 70 years old.
  2. His net worth is 4.5 billion dollars.
  3. He has five children.
  4. Trump completed the development of Trump Tower in 1983.

General Facts about Clinton

  1. She is 68 years old.
  2. She is married to former president Bill Clinton.
  3. She has one child. Her name is Chelsea Clinton.
  4. Hillary served as secretary of state from 2009-2013.
